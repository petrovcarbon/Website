Frequently asked questions

My flowers' petals are falling off, what do i do?

It is important to pay attention and care to your plants. In such case you can try adding rich soil and monitor its dampness.

What causes leaves to become pale?

The most common reason for leaves turning pale is that they're not getting enough sunlight. Try moving it close to a source of light.

How do i change pots?

Water the plant first, that way the roots aren't pulled when you change its pot. Go up one size and fill around with fresh soil.
